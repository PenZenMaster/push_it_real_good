People Also Ask

How do I know if bees are nesting in my home?

Look for increased bee activity, buzzing sounds in walls, or visible nests near eaves or vents.

Can bees damage a house?

Yes, bee nests in walls can attract moisture and pests, damaging insulation, drywall, or wood.

What should I do if I see a bee swarm?

Stay calm, do not disturb the swarm, and call a licensed bee removal expert immediately.
