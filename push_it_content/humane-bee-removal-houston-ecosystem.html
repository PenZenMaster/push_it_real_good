People Also Ask

Why is humane bee removal important?

It preserves bee populations which are essential for pollination and environmental balance.

Is humane bee removal safe?

Yes, professionals use non-lethal methods to relocate bees without endangering people or pets.

Where are bees relocated to?

They’re typically rehomed to managed hives or rural apiaries where they can thrive.
